CEO
Tamara Johnson

Tamara Johnson is an experienced interior designer with 15 years of experience. Driven by her passion for interior design, she takes pride in providing the best service to her clientele. As a designer/CEO, her goals include capturing the clients vision to the best of her ability and ensuring that her company is always working to their best ability. Graduating from the University of Auckland, Tamara has acquired a Bachelor of Commerce/ Bachelor of Fine Arts Conjoint. Giving her and edge ove her competition. Tamara founded the company 10 years ago with the help of her now Managing Director David Quinto and Rosalin Darwin. In her spare time, Tamara enjoys playing the Sims series, which allows her to hone her design skills with the endless design possibilities of the game.
