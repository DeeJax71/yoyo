Restaurants

Project Elegance

One of the first projects undertaken by Captive Interiors. Project Elegance, showcases a beautiful combination of both nature and minimalism, allowing for natural light to seep into the environment. A matching color scheme blesses customers throughout the Restaurant allowing them to enjoy their foods whilst be surrounding by beauty.

Project Lineup

Project Lineup, was designed with the intention of keeping everything in line. From the tiling to the perfect placement of the tables in front of the windows. From the bar to the table tops everything has a design containing a line of some sort, to complete the overall theme of keeping things in line.

Project Nature

Project Nature, designed with the inteion of incorporating as much nature as possible. Green accents, green plants, green napkins, this project screams NATURE! Every last detail was meticulously chosen to resemble a natural environment within the modern era.

Hotels

Project Opus

Project Opus is Captivate Interiors first dive into desigining the lobby of a hotel. With the intention of creating a sleek, elegant and welcoming lobby for guests, this project has blown the client out of the waters. the hidden artificial lights add to the sleek factor.

Project Transfixion

Project Transfixion, creates an open space to welcome the guests. Around the corner there is another waiting zone that is shielded from the entrance to the building. The lighting within the room helps to accentuate the golden metallic walls, whilst also adding a bit of warmth to the atmosphere

Project Cajun

Project Cajun, encompasses a welcoming feeling. Using warm lights, an open floor plan, with a waiting area allowing multiple guests to converse whilst taking in the beautiful surroundings. The multitude of windows allows for more natural light to the flood the room during the day.

Offices

Project Lamentra

Project Lamentra is the first office interior project that Captivate Interiors has completed. It is an open plan layout with plentiful amounts of natural lighting to flood the workspace. All desks have the users best interest in mind, being equipped with a standing desk, and an ergonomic office chair.

Project Elevate

Project Elevate, navy blue and white with a hint of purple. Built to enable as much symmetry as possible this office space has a clean look to it. The lighting within the area is clean and allows the workers to work. The desks allow for easy socialising which is a key feature of this workspace.

Project Luxury

Project Luxury, is designed to resemble law firms as featured in many tv shows. The inviting windows allow for people to see the occupants of the room but not hear what is being said within the room. The lighting compliments the color scheme by matching the colors of the furniture and other items.

Homes

Project Tamara

Project Tamara, is the first project Captivate Interiors has taken. Being the CEO, tamara wanted to allow clients to see what her and her team were capable of doing to get their name out in the world. This lounge features many minimalist designs such as a wall mounted tv allowing for cables to be hidden aswell as the utility of shelves lining the walls to allow for more storage.

Project Lobby

Project Lobby, transforms a house into a home. The way everything has been placed is to compliment that many windows that surround the lounge, allowing for a modicum of privacy in this open space. from the flawless transition between indoors and outdoors by using the same floor material to the staircase that leads to the second level, everything in this home is well thought out to meet the clients needs.

Project BWB

Project BWB, standing for Black White Brown. Captures the true essence of a welcoming home, moving the focus from the TV to the guests within the home. With the space provided in the room, it is easy to rearrange the place to accomodate for more guests or furniture. The colow scheme compliments the whole setup by ensuring there is synergy between all the colors.
